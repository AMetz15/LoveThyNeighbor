Recieving Food

Necessary Documentation

Patrons need to bring documentation proving their current address and identifications (a Drivers License, a Social Security Card or a bill showing your name and address).

Once Every 30 Days

Patrons are only allowed to use our services once every 30 days. If more frequent assistance is required, contact 211 for a referral.

Our Service Region

Patrons must have a permanent address within our service region to qualify.
